## Overview

This poster is a quick-reference guide to Digital Literacy — covering what it means, which tools support effective digital work, and how to stay safe online. It was created as part of an awareness initiative aimed at students and early internet users.

---

## What is Digital Literacy?

Digital literacy is the ability to find, evaluate, and communicate information using digital platforms. It goes beyond basic computer use — it involves thinking critically about online content, using tools responsibly, and participating effectively in digital spaces.

## Safe Internet Practices

- **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** — adds a second layer of security beyond your password.
- **Use strong, unique passphrases** — avoid reusing passwords across platforms.
- **Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money** — UPI PINs are only needed to send money. Any request for your PIN while "receiving" is a scam.
